    We are your connection with the sustainable progress

    Our Mission

    Generating local sky horizon from an image by segmentation and using the information of camera optics angle of elevation for each lowest sky pixel is calculated for analysis for solar energy potential in urban setting.

    Our Idea

    Image of the user's vicinity is captured by using the smart phone and uploaded to the server, after which it is processed and an image with the masked sky region is given as output by apllying deep learning techniques. An additional report with statistics and visualizations of solar energy potential for development of efficicent solar energy policy is generated.

    Our Vision

    To provide and estimation of solar energy required for generating electricity for various application domains that include agricultural sector, industry, solar plants, residential purposes as well. Improving the quality of electricity as it being one of the aspects for a better future.
